What We're All About

Here at WaxWorks we know the candle is a symbol of enlightenment and truth. Our mission is to illuminate young minds, and blaze a radiant path through the darkness. All of our profits go directly to community projects that focus on education. We contribute to new, student-founded initiatives whenever possible, and we also support existing educational programs that we believe show merit in helping the most underserved communities and demographics.

Come check us out!

We currently have 2 education centers in Chicago, and Louisville that provide after-school candlemaking workshops. The main goal is to provide constructive, safe environments for the children attending, but we also offer adult classes, as well as open use of the facilities for local artists and hobbyists.
